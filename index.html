About the Workshop

The rapid advancement of generative AI has transformed the landscape of digital art creation, enabling new forms of collaboration between human artists and intelligent systems. This workshop brings together researchers, artists, and practitioners to explore the intersection of human creativity and AI capabilities in generative art.

We aim to foster discussions on novel graphics methods, interactive systems, and applications that leverage human-AI collaboration to push the boundaries of artistic expression. Join us to share insights, present cutting-edge research, and envision the future of co-creative art-making.
